ParaEQ 301 — a parametric EQ that finishes the sound instead of just correcting it. One plugin: AU · VST3 · Standalone (macOS). Bought once, yours.

1. EQ that finishes the sound, instead of just correcting it

When your EQ gets the frequencies right but the track still sounds flat and digital, ParaEQ shapes it with the same parametric bands — plus saturation and shaper stages in the chain. The move that used to sound corrected now sounds finished: weight and warmth, not just a curve.

2. Nothing hidden — A/B the colour against clean

Flip “Linear EQ only” and compare the clean parametric EQ against the full chain. You hear exactly what ParaEQ adds, and keep only what serves the song. No faith required — a plugin confident enough to let you hear it switched off earns trust.

3. Make a frequency breathe — the thing a normal EQ cannot do

Bring up the resonator cores and Motion — saturating bandpass resonance, parametric pumping, an LFO across the bands — and a flat element starts to move and sing in a way ordinary EQ simply can’t.

Put it on a track you know and reach for “Linear EQ only” first. You hear the clean parametric, then the full chain — so from the first session you decide, by ear, exactly how much colour earns its place. And if it doesn’t earn one, a refund is a click away. The risk is mine, not yours.

Buy it. Run it on a real mix. Keep it only if it makes the record better.
